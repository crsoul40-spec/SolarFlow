## Template 3: The 72-Hour "Financing & Tax Credit" Breakdown

**When to send:** 72 hours after delivering the proposal.

**Purpose:** Address the two biggest decision blockers—"How do I pay for this?" and "How does the tax credit actually work?"—before the homeowner goes silent.

---

**Subject:** How the 30% federal tax credit works for your solar project

**Body:**

Hi [HOMEOWNER FIRST NAME],

When homeowners look at a solar proposal for the first time, the two biggest questions are usually:

1. "How does the tax credit actually work?"
2. "What are my financing options?"

I want to make sure you have clear answers to both.

**The Federal Solar Tax Credit (ITC)**

The Inflation Reduction Act gives homeowners a tax credit worth **30% of the total cost** of a new solar system. For your [SYSTEM SIZE] kW system, that's an estimated **$[ITC AMOUNT]** off your federal taxes.

Here's how it works in practice:
- The credit is applied when you file your federal tax return for the year the system is installed.
- It's a **dollar-for-dollar reduction** of the taxes you owe — not a deduction, a credit.
- If your tax liability in year one doesn't cover the full credit, you can roll the remainder into the following tax year.

*I always recommend consulting your tax advisor for guidance specific to your situation.*

**Financing Options**

Most of our homeowners choose one of these paths:

| Option | How It Works |
|--------|-------------|
| **Solar loan** | $0 down, fixed monthly payments of ~$[MONTHLY PAYMENT]/mo for [TERM] years. Many homeowners apply the ITC refund toward principal in year one. |
| **Cash purchase** | Pay $[NET COST] after the tax credit. Highest long-term ROI with no interest costs. |
| **Lease / PPA** | Available in [STATE IF APPLICABLE]. Lower savings but zero upfront cost and no maintenance responsibility. |

Would it help to schedule a 15-minute call to walk through which option makes the most sense for your household? I'm here to answer questions, not pressure you.

Just reply to this email or call me at [YOUR PHONE NUMBER].

Best,
